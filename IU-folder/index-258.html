Degrees & Majors

Hundreds of academic programs across multiple campuses and online

Our degrees for dreamers, doers, and leaders will prepare you for whatever comes next. You can study practically anything here, and many of our programs are among the world’s best.

Whichever on-campus or online degree you choose, you’ll earn an Indiana University degree. It connects you with more than 690,000 living IU graduates—and it shows you’re ready to shine.
